Welcome to Vera Glo! This space was created with artists, dreamers, and curious souls in mind. Here, I share pieces of my heart—my religious artwork, my wild and whimsical ideas, and the thoughts and inspirations that have carried me through life. What once lived only in my imagination now lives here, on the page, for you to explore. I believe that everyone can find a little beauty, meaning, or spark of joy within these works. More than anything, I hope you can feel the love, care, and deep passion poured into every piece of art you see.
